Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AKB Football Academy

You can enroll by filling out the registration form on our Contact page. One of our staff members will get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your child's place and provide further details.

We accept players from age 6 to 18. Our three programs are Junior Stars (Ages 6-9), Youth Development (Ages 10-14), and Elite Squad (Ages 15-18).

Players need football boots, shin guards, a water bottle, and comfortable training kit. The academy provides training bibs and footballs during sessions.

Monthly fees vary by program. Junior Stars is KSh 2,500/month, Youth Development is KSh 3,500/month, and Elite Squad is KSh 5,000/month. A one-time registration fee of KSh 1,000 applies to all new players.

All training sessions are held at our main facility in Nairobi. Exact location details are provided upon enrollment. We have two full-size pitches and one small-sided pitch available.
