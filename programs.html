Our Programs

Structured training for every age and skill level

Junior Stars (Ages 6-9)

An introduction to football fundamentals through fun, games-based sessions. Focus on ball control, coordination, and a love for the game.

  • 2 sessions per week
  • 1-hour training
  • Small-sided games

Youth Development (Ages 10-14)

Structured technical and tactical training to build well-rounded players. Includes positional training and introduction to competitive matches.

  • 3 sessions per week
  • 1.5-hour training
  • Weekend friendly matches

Elite Squad (Ages 15-18)

High-intensity training for competitive players aiming to play at a higher level, including league matches and performance analysis.

  • 4 sessions per week
  • 2-hour training
  • League competition
